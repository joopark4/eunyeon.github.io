## 1. Embedding Images
You can easily embed images using Markdown syntax. Just place them in the `assets/images/` folder.

## 3. Providing Download Links
For your private projects, you can provide a direct link to download the release (executable, zip file, etc.). 

If the file is **under 100MB**, you can upload it to your repository's `/assets/downloads/` folder.
If it's larger, we recommend linking to a Google Drive or S3 bucket.
